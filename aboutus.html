Establishment

Shenen Gibe is the only hospital found in the Boche Bore kebele, which was established in 2004, E.C and it became a general hospital in 2010. It has a total of 215 workers, and it aims to increase this number to 399. There are 3 specialists, 15 doctors and 60 nurses that work in the hospital.
